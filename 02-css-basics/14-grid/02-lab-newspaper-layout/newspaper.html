Feria de Candon 2025 Highlights

Electric Float Parade Lights Up Candon

This year's Feria de Candon dazzled locals and visitors alike with an amazing electric-float parade across Candon City. Dozens of floats crafted by barangays from the second district of Ilocos Sur lit up the streets with elaborate designs representing culture, history, and creativity. Thousands flocked to witness the vibrant showcase, highlighting the craftsmanship and unity of Ilocanos.

Economic Boost & Growing Tourism

Feria de Candon continues to uplift local tourism and business. Vendors, artisans, and small entrepreneurs experienced increased sales during the event. The festival’s rising popularity positions Candon City as one of Ilocos Sur’s cultural hotspots, attracting more local and international visitors each year.

OTOP Trade Fair Highlights

The revived One Town, One Product (OTOP) showcase featured handcrafted goods, delicacies, and agricultural products. The fair gave locals an opportunity to support Ilocano artisans and entrepreneurs.

Señorita Feria Pageantry

The “Señorita Feria de Candon” pageant brought elegance to the parade as municipal representatives rode the illuminated floats. The competition blended beauty, culture, and civic representation.

Historical Roots

Feria de Candon traces its origins to 1892 during the Primera Feria Exposición de la Provincia de Ilocos Sur. Today, it continues the legacy of trade, community celebration, and cultural pride.
