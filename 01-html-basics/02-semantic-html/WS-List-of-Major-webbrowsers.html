List of Major Web Browsers

Google Chrome
A free web browser developed by Google, first released in 2008. Known for its speed, simplicity, and tight integration with Google services.
Mozilla Firefox
A free and open-source browser developed by Mozilla, launched in 2004. Praised for privacy features and customization options.
Apple Safari
The default browser for Apple devices—iPhone, iPad, and Mac—optimized for performance and energy efficiency.
Brave
A Chromium-based browser released in 2016 that blocks ads and trackers by default, rewarding users with its own token system.
Arc Browser
A modern Chromium-based browser released in 2023 by The Browser Company, focusing on productivity and fresh UI design.
