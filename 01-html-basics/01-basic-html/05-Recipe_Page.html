Filipino Chicken Adobo

Adobo is one of the most iconic Filipino dishes — savory, slightly tangy, and packed with flavor. This simple recipe will guide you in making the perfect adobo at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté the marinated chicken until lightly browned.
  3. Add the marinade, bay leaves, and peppercorns, then simmer for 30–40 minutes.
  4. Adjust seasoning to taste, and cook until the sauce is slightly thickened.
